'Main Kya Hoon - What Am I'

At birth, the Self is a blank slate, which is then etched with external labels. It is others who inform the individual’s idea of who they are and the individual grows to know this as the Truth. ‘Main Kya Hoon’ is an existential inquiry into the real meaning of being.
