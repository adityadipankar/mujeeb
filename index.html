About

Mujeeb Shaikh is a self-taught Hindi and Urdu poet based out of Mumbai. He worked as an electronic music producer for a decade before joining a Sufi-fusion band as a drummer. His poetry draws significant inspiration from the works of Amir Khusrau, Mir Taqi Mir, Jaun Elia, Qateel Shifai, Javed Akhtar and Gulzar. Mujeeb’s personal style of writing be it songs, shayaris or ghazals is a very open hearted and non adulterated take on romance ranging from unrequited love to that of a forlorn lover to angst ridden love to that of a mystic.
