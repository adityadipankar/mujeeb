About

Mujeeb Shaikh is a self-taught Hindi and Urdu poet based out of Mumbai. He worked as an electronic music producer for a decade before joining a Sufi-fusion band as a drummer. His poetry draws significant inspiration from the works of Amir Khusrau, Mir Taqi Mir, Jaun Elia, Qateel Shifai, Javed Akhtar and Gulzar.
Mujeeb’s personal style of writing be it songs, shayaris or ghazals is a very open hearted and non adulterated take on romance ranging from unrequited love to that of a forlorn lover to angst ridden love to that of a mystic.

'Main Kya Hoon'

At birth, the Self is a blank slate, which is then etched with external labels. It is others who inform the individual’s idea of who they are and the individual grows to know this as the Truth. ‘Main Kya Hoon’ is an existential inquiry into the real meaning of being.
